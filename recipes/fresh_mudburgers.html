Fresh Mudburgers

Mud and burgers combined, and somehow they're fresh? I don't know how it works, but it came from James and the Giant Peach by Roald Dahl so you'll have to figure it out. No disrespect to the GOAT.

Ingredients

  • 7 oz of semi-sweet chocolate, broken into small pieces
  • 4 tbsp (1/2 stick) of unsalted butter
  • 5 tbsp of light corn syrup
  • 3 oz of slivered almonds
  • 6 plain vanilla cookies or graham crackers, finely crushed
  • 1 oz of Rice Krispies (Rice Bubbles for the people down under)
  • A few drops of vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp of water
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 oz of slivered almonds, finely chopped
